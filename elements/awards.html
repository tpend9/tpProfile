The Braille Sensor device that helps the visually impaired turn Braille into sound. Worn like a glove the devices main electrical components are in a small compartment at the top of the hand, leading to the sensors at the tip of the fingers. These sensors pick up the Braille and translate it into a specific language. Through this project, Tremayne aims to assist the visually impaired speed up their learning process.
